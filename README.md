# Social Media and Politics in Tamil Nadu

## Objective
To analyze how political content on Instagram and YouTube generates public engagement and how algorithms influence political visibility.

## Data
- instagram_clean.csv (48 posts)
- youtube_clean.csv (50 videos)

## Methods
- Engagement rate calculation
- Grouped analysis by content type
- Visualization of engagement patterns

## Key Findings
- Troll and meme content receives significantly higher engagement.
- Official political communication performs poorly.
- Algorithms amplify emotional and sensational content.

## Implications
Digital politics is shifting from ideological discourse to performative, attention-based politics.
